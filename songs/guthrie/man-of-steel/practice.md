# Man Of Steel — Practice Plan
## Guthrie Govan

### Phase 1: Foundation (Sessions 1-10)
**Goal:** Nail the prerequisite techniques and easiest sections

**Daily routine (30 min):**
- 5 min: Technique warm-up
- 10 min: Section P at 50% tempo
- 10 min: Section I at 50% tempo
- 5 min: Review previous material

**Milestone:** Play foundation sections cleanly at 50% tempo
**Sections covered:** Section P, Section N, Section O, Section K, Section I

### Phase 2: Building (Sessions 11-20)
**Goal:** Tackle medium-difficulty sections and start connecting parts

**Daily routine (30 min):**
- 5 min: Technique warm-up
- 10 min: New section at 40% tempo
- 10 min: Review foundation sections at increasing tempo
- 5 min: Transitions between learned sections

**Milestone:** Play all learned sections at 60% tempo
**Sections covered:** Section C, Section E, Section J, Section M, Section G

### Phase 3: Peak (Sessions 21-38)
**Goal:** Conquer the hardest sections

**Daily routine (30 min):**
- 5 min: Technique warm-up focusing on speed
- 15 min: Hardest section, slow and methodical
- 10 min: All sections individually at current max tempo

**Milestone:** Play all sections individually at 70% tempo
**Sections covered:** Section H, Section A, Section D, Section L, Section B, Section F

### Phase 4: Assembly (Sessions 39-46)
**Goal:** Connect all sections into the complete solo

**Daily routine (30 min):**
- 5 min: Technique warm-up
- 10 min: Weakest transitions
- 15 min: Full solo run-throughs

**Milestone:** Play the complete solo at 80% tempo

### Phase 5: Performance (Sessions 47+)
**Goal:** Bring the solo to full tempo with expression and confidence

**Daily routine (30 min):**
- 5 min: Technique warm-up
- 5 min: Weakest section at current max tempo
- 20 min: Full solo run-throughs, increasing tempo by 5 bpm when 3 clean passes achieved

**Milestone:** Full solo at 100% tempo (128 bpm)

### Tempo Milestones
| Phase | Target | BPM |
|-------|--------|-----|
| Foundation | 50% | 64 bpm |
| Building | 60% | 76 bpm |
| Peak | 70% | 89 bpm |
| Assembly | 80% | 102 bpm |
| Performance | 100% | 128 bpm |
