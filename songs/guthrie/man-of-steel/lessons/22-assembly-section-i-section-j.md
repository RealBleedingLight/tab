# Lesson 22: Assembly — Section I + Section J
**Phase:** Assembly
**Prerequisites:** the lesson for Section I, the lesson for Section J
**Estimated time:** 20 minutes
**You'll need:** Guitar, metronome, tuner

## Goal
Connect Section I and Section J into a seamless passage at 40% tempo (51 bpm). The transitions between sections should feel natural — no hesitation, no gear-shifting.

## Warm-Up (5 min)
- Quick run-through of each section individually at 40% tempo

## Concept: Connecting Sections
The hardest part of playing a solo isn't any individual section — it's the transitions between them. When you practiced each section in isolation, you always started from a "ready" position. Now you need to arrive at each section mid-flow, with your hand already in motion. Focus on:
- **Position shifts:** Know exactly where your hand needs to move between sections
- **Timing continuity:** The beat doesn't pause between sections — keep the metronome running
- **Dynamic arc:** Each section has its own energy level — practice matching the dynamics at the join point

## Steps

### Step 1: Review Each Section
Play through Section I and Section J separately, one time each.
- **Do this:** One pass each at 51 bpm. Note how each section ends and begins.
- **Listen for:** Confidence in each section individually.
- **Repeat:** Once through each.

### Step 2: The Transition
Focus on the join point: the last 2 bars of Section I into the first 2 bars of Section J (around bar 39).
- **Do this:** Loop just this 4-bar transition 10 times. Pay attention to position shifts, dynamic changes, and timing.
- **Listen for:** Smooth flow through the transition. No hesitation, no gear-shifting.
- **Repeat:** 10 loops of the transition.

### Step 3: Full Combined Run
Play from the start of Section I through the end of Section J.
- **Do this:** Metronome at 51 bpm. Play the full combined passage.
- **Listen for:** The transition should feel invisible — like one continuous phrase.
- **Repeat:** 3 clean passes of the full combined section.

## Checkpoint
- [ ] Can you play Section I into Section J without stopping?
- [ ] Is the transition smooth with no hesitation?
- [ ] Can you do 3 clean passes at 40% tempo?

**All checked?** Move to Lesson 23
**Not yet?** Identify which transition is weakest. Isolate the last 2 bars of the first section and the first 2 bars of the next — loop just that transition 10 times.
