# Man of Steel — Practice Plan
## Guthrie Govan Solo

Context: Leo is intermediate level, comfortable with barre chords and pentatonic scales. This is a challenging solo.

### Phase 1: Foundation (Week 1-2)
**Goal:** Nail the melodic sections and core techniques

**Daily routine (30 min):**
- 5 min: Vibrato exercises — practice on B string fret 15, aim for even, controlled oscillation
- 5 min: Bend accuracy — bend B string fret 15 to match fret 17 pitch, then try 1.5 step bends
- 10 min: Section A (bars 1-8) at 50% tempo — focus on phrasing and feel, not speed
- 10 min: Section F (bars 49-56) at 50% tempo — sustained notes with vibrato

**Milestone:** Play sections A and F cleanly at 60% tempo with good vibrato

### Phase 2: Legato Development (Week 3-4)
**Goal:** Build legato strength and fluidity

**Daily routine (30 min):**
- 5 min: Legato warm-up — 4-note-per-string patterns, ascending and descending, all strings
- 10 min: Section B (bars 9-16) at 50% tempo — focus on even note volume
- 10 min: Section E (bars 37-48) at 50% tempo — sustained bends and dynamics
- 5 min: Review Sections A and F at increasing tempo

**Milestone:** Play sections A, B, E, F cleanly at 70% tempo

### Phase 3: Speed Building (Week 5-8)
**Goal:** Tackle the fast sections

**Daily routine (40 min):**
- 5 min: Alternate picking warm-up — chromatic exercise 1-2-3-4 across all strings
- 15 min: Section C (bars 17-24) — start at 40% tempo, isolate the hardest 2-bar phrases
- 15 min: Section D (bars 25-36) — break into 4-bar chunks, practice each separately
- 5 min: Run through sections A, B, E, F at current best tempo

**Milestone:** All sections playable at 60% tempo

### Phase 4: Assembly (Week 9-10)
**Goal:** Play the full solo

**Daily routine (30 min):**
- 5 min: Warm-up with the hardest 4 bars
- 20 min: Full run-through, starting at 60% tempo, increase 5bpm when clean
- 5 min: Isolate and drill any bars that broke down

**Milestone:** Full solo at 80% tempo

### Phase 5: Performance (Week 11+)
**Goal:** Full speed with expression

- Play along with the recording
- Focus on dynamics — the solo isn't just notes, it's the crescendo from melodic to intense and back
- Record yourself and compare to Guthrie's phrasing

### Tempo Targets
| Phase | Target Tempo | Notes |
|-------|-------------|-------|
| Foundation | 50-60% | Melodic sections only |
| Legato | 60-70% | Adding sections B and E |
| Speed | 40-60% | Sections C and D start slower |
| Assembly | 60-80% | Full solo connected |
| Performance | 80-100% | With recording |

### Practice Tips
- **Never practice mistakes.** If you're making errors, slow down immediately.
- **Use a metronome** for everything. Phone apps work fine (Pro Metronome, etc.)
- **Record yourself** weekly to track progress — you'll hear improvements you can't feel in the moment.
- **Take breaks** — if your hand is tense or hurting, stop. Tension causes injury and kills speed.
